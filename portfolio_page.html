Key Findings

Channel Quality
+14pp

AQX Digital Channel Advantage

Policies acquired through the AQX digital quoting platform renew at 82% vs. 68% for Direct Web. The gap holds across all cohort quarters since AQX launched in 2023Q3, suggesting structural customer quality differences rather than seasonal variation.

Cross-Sell Gap
28,000+

Quoted but Not Attached for Renters

Over 28,000 policyholders were offered renters insurance at quote and declined. At the $280/yr average renters premium, this represents $7.8M in unrealized annual revenue -- the single largest near-term revenue opportunity identified.

Model Output
1.59x

Top Decile Lapse Lift

The XGBoost gradient boosting model scores all 90,000 policies by lapse probability. The top decile shows a 40% actual lapse rate vs. 25.2% for the full book -- a 1.59x lift enabling cost-effective outreach prioritization before the 60-day renewal window.

Billing Signal
#2 SHAP

Payment Consistency Predicts Lapse

Payment consistency score is the second-strongest SHAP predictor of lapse (behind acquisition channel). Policies with missed payments or NSF events show materially lower renewal rates, enabling a rules-based lapse alert deployable without a model serving layer.
